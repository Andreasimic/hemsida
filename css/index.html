Welcome to Apartment Sunset

The property is an 8-minute walk from the beach. Apartment Sunset has a balcony with sea views, a garden and a terrace.

Here you are staying in Makarska, close to Makarska Bus Station and less than 1 km from St. Mark's Cathedral.

The air-conditioned accommodation is 1.3 km from Ratac Beach, and guests benefit from private parking available on site and free WiFi. The apartment has 2 bedrooms, 1 bathroom, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a dining area, a fully equipped kitchen, and a patio with garden views. Popular points of interest nearby include Makarska Main Square, Sveti Petar Lighthouse and Makarska Harbour.

The nearest airport is Brac Airport, 28 km from Apartment Sunset.
